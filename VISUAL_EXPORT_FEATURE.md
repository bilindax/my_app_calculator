## How It Works
1. When you click **"Insert Table to AutoCAD"** (in Quantities Tab or Summary Tab):
2. The app generates the standard data table.
3. It then uses `matplotlib` to generate two PNG images (charts) in a temporary folder.
4. These images are inserted into AutoCAD as **Raster Images** to the right of the summary table.
